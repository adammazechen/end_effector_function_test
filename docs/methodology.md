# Methodology

## 1. Problem Setup

1. Task definition: the default grippers for UFACTORY xArm 7 are not suited for retrieving orthopedic plates due to their dimensions and design, thus new gripper designs are required. This experiment measures their effectiveness at completing varied grasp tasks.

2. Input: three gripper designs (Figure 1.) created with AutoCAD, and printed using PLA and enhanced with rubber padding. They are installed to the UFACTORY xArm 7 and tested on three grasping tests (TODO: add figure illustration).
![Gripper Designs](../assets/images/end_effectors_photo.png)
*Figure 1: gripper design g_0, g_1 and g_2 (left to right)*

3. Output: each experiment is assessed by the success in three steps of motion, including removing the orthopedic plate from tray, transporting it to the drop off box, and placing it in the drop off box. The nominal data are collected and organized in [Metric.csv](../results/metric.csv), and video records of the experiments can be found in [Recording Links](../results/recording_links.md).
